Dear Flicstarters,

We are pleased to announce that Flicstart has joined forces with Tugg.com!

Flicstart was founded nearly four years ago with a simple idea: that audiences should be able to choose the content they see at their local movie theater. Tugg continues to build on that vision, and we could not be more thrilled to be part of the Tugg family.

To continue attending and hosting awesome on-demand movie screenings, simply head over to Tugg.com to browse existing events or create your own!

Thank you for being part of our community.

Jeff, Andrew, Paul, Mayda, and Joe
